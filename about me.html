ABOUT ME

Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

My name is Nur Suhailah Binti Mohd Sham, and I'm a passionate degree student of Information Science (Honours) Library Management based in UiTM Merbok, Kedah, with a zest for life and a love for all things creative. Born and raised in Tawau, Sabah until my age seven years old. After that my family and I moved to Kedah and we lived in Sungai Petani until now with lot of joy and cherish. I've always been driven by curiosity and an insatiable desire to learn.

As a student, I find joy in my study journey, blending my technical skills with a dash of creativity. Whether it's a bit challenging, I strive to infuse my study things with a sense of purpose and passion. Outside of my student life, I'm keen on badminton sport which all my family members will play together during our leisure time. I'm also a dedicated in listening music, reading books and an enthusiastic in baking. This website is a glimpse into my world a mosaic of my experiences, interests, and the people who have shaped me along the way. Join me as I share my journey and the little things that make life extraordinary.
